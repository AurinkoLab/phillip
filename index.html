Fun Math Facts I Love

π

The Magic of Pi

Pi (π) is approximately 3.14159 and goes on forever without repeating! It's the ratio of a circle's circumference to its diameter. I've memorized the first 50 digits!

∞

Infinity and Beyond

Did you know there are different sizes of infinity? The infinity of counting numbers is smaller than the infinity of all real numbers. Mind-blowing, right?

F

Fibonacci Sequence

The Fibonacci sequence (0, 1, 1, 2, 3, 5, 8, 13...) appears everywhere in nature! You can find it in flower petals, pinecones, and even galaxies!

4D

The Fourth Dimension

We live in a 3D world, but mathematicians can work with any number of dimensions! A 4D cube is called a tesseract, and it looks super cool when projected into 3D.
